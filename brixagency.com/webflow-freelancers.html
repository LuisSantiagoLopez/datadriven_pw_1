Frequently Asked Questions

Webflow Freelancers FAQs

Take a look at some of the most common questions client asks us regarding Webflow freelancers, Webflow agencies, and more. Can't find the question you are looking for? Please Get in touch.



What is the cost to hire a Webflow freelancer?

Webflow is one of the most popular website builders of 2021, and because of that, the market demand for experienced Webflow developers is very high at the moment.

If we take a look at the public data from the Webflow Experts program, the program for pre-vetted Webflow Experts, the average hourly rates range from $100 to $200 USD per hour, which means a full website design (of around 5-10 pages) could cost between $10 and $25K USD, depending on the freelancer rates.

If, on the other hand, you are considering hiring a Webflow agency, the costs can vary significantly. However, this investment will lead to a considerable improvement in the quality of your website.



How long does it take to develop a Webflow website?

Each Webflow website is unique and has its own complexity, however, Webflow is getting very popular because of its agile form of development, which usually requires considerably less development work than other tech stacks or frameworks.

Based on our Webflow Experts, we see an average of 4 to 8 weeks for a small Webflow marketing website with around 5-10 pages, however, this can be less or more depending on your specific website scope and requirements.



How much does it cost to maintain a Webflow website?

Unlike other tech stacks or CMS like WordPress, Webflow doesn't require any kind of manual maintenance or security updates, because everything is managed automatically by the Webflow platform itself. Thanks to this, the maintenance fees are very low.

The only cost associated with running a Webflow website is the payment for the Webflow hosting plan, and the Webflow account plan, which depending on which one you had chosen, can vary between a range of $25 to $100 USD per month.
