What is the best process to hire a designer?

Design is a very extensive discipline, so while one designer can be a great fit for one role that requires a certain set of skill sets, it can also be a bad fit for another one.

When you are looking to hire a full-time designer for your team, you should properly understand the daily tasks that the designer will be performing to hire the right designer, so you can focus on the skills required to successfully complete those tasks.

If you are looking for a more in-depth answer to this question, you can read our How to hire a graphic designer article that answers this question in detail.



How long does it take to hire a web designer?

Web design is one of the most demanded skills in the design industry in 2022, so hiring a web designer may take some more time than a traditional graphic designer or a brand designer, taking into account that the pool of talent is smaller.

Taking into account the average hiring time from the LinkedIn Economic Graph study, a Web Designer role is typically filled out in a median time of 39 days, however, based on our experience, depending on the skills and specific requirements, past customers have experimented hiring times in the range of 3 - 4 months using traditional hiring methods.

By working with our web design recruiting agency, we can help you onboard the right web designer in 2 to 4 weeks. If you would like to learn more of How to hire a web designer, we recommend you to check out our latest article.



How to hire a UI / UX designer?

Unlike design areas like branding or graphic design where design aesthethic and look and feel is one of the core goals, UI/UX design is a discipline where the ease of use of the design, as well as the user research and prototyping, are major points towards building a successful design or not.

Considering this, when hiring a UI / UX designer, it's important to analyze not only the design aesthetics, but the actual design UX flow and thought process behind all the design work.

That is why it's important to understand how to hire a ui/ux designer, otherwise, the team member hired may not be up to the design quality that you are looking for.



How much does it cost to hire a designer?

Design is a pretty variate industry, so the annual cost for hiring a full-time designer can vary a lot depending on the skillset that you are looking for.

For example, hiring a UX designer can be quite different (financially wise) from hiring a graphic designer, so you should first get a clear set of skills that you are looking to hire, and based on that, you will get a better sense of the cost.

If you want a detailed breakdown of the different design types and average salaries for each, we recommend you take a look at our report on How much does it cost to hire a graphic designer.
