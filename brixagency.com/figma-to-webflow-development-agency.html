How much does a Figma to Webflow project cost?

The cost of a Figma to Webflow development project can vary greatly depending on the scope of the project, and the Webflow agency that is working on it.

Generally, the cost for a simple project with 1-3 pages can range from $2,500 to $5,000 USD, while more complex projects can easily cost more than $10,000 USD.

Other factors that affect the cost of a Figma to Webflow development project include the complexity of the Webflow build, the level of expertise of the Webflow development team, and the timeline of the project.

î —

How much does it cost to hire a Figma to Webflow developer?

The cost of hiring a Webflow developer to do an hourly Figma to Webflow development project can vary significantly depending on the developer's experience and expertise.

For a junior Webflow developer, you can expect an hourly rate in between $20 and 30 USD, however, a senior Webflow developer with many years of experience and in-depth knowledge of advanced Webflow features can easily cost $75 to $150 per hour.

If you are looking to hire Webflow Freelancers by the our, we recommend you to get in touch with us. On the other side, if you are looking to hire an agency to do a fully Webflow design & development project, we recommend you to reach out to our Webflow agency.

î —

How long does a Figma to Webflow project takes?

The time it takes to develop a Figma to Webflow project depends a lot on the size and complexity of the Figma design.

A simple project can take around 2 to 4 weeks, while more complicated projects that require advanced animations, complex 3rd-party integrations, and multiple CMS features can easily take between 2 to 3 months.
