10 Machine Learning Website Designs for Inspiration in 2023

Explore our hand-picked selection of the best machine learning website designs to find cool ideas for your ML startup.

Explore websites
 Table of contents

With the increasing popularity of machine learning, businesses are looking for ways to incorporate this technology into their products. However, designing a website that effectively communicates its features can be challenging.

We would like to help with machine learning website design inspiration for your next project, so we’ve compiled a list of the top 10 machine learning website designs that can be really inspiring for you. Whether you are a designer, developer, or business owner, these websites are sure to blow your mind with their creativity and stunning designs.

1. Scale - Machine Learning Website Design Inspiration

Taking the lead in first place 🥇, we have the Scale website, one of the biggest players in the machine learning and artificial intelligence industry.

We’re sure that many people were anticipating this website, and with good reason: The Scale website is a reference in the ML web design industry. The dark mode takes the spotlight in this design, not only with amazing 3D abstract shapes but also with glass elements combined with duo-tone images and interactive isometric illustrations.

The website also incorporates sections in light mode that grab the attention of their users. You’ll definitely feel that this company is at the cutting edge of machine learning website design.

Take a look at the amazing Scale website here.

2. GenerativeAI - Machine Learning App Website Design Inspiration

Securing the second spot 🥈, we have GenerativeAI, a machine learning app based on the concept of generative images used by players such as DALL-E, Midjourney, or Stable Diffusion. This is our favorite ML website design created by the amazing team at BRIX Templates.

The website uses many images generated by its AI and takes its product images as a model to represent every step of the way it works, with simple screenshots and product tours. The layout is clean and professional, with big sections, large fonts, and vibrant colors.

GenerativeAI is an ideal machine learning web design that every ML-powered product would want to have. Fortunately, we can help you design a website like this — you just need to send us a message.

Would you like to see the GenerativeAI website? Please click here.

3. Raycast - Machine Learning Platform Web Design Inspiration

Coming in at number three 🥉, we have the visually cutting-edge website of Raycast, an extendable launcher for macOS that lets you complete tasks, calculate, share common links, and much more.

So far on the podium, we have presented dark mode machine learning design websites, and for good reason: dark mode pays off. It’s not just a matter of design, but of quality design trends. The Raycast website is incredible, with many product images created with different animations and interactions for users.

The style is very clean and uses red and pink as accent colors, the elements are small, and every illustration is based on the product, which is a great example of how you can use information about your product to make your website impressive.

Sounds really good, doesn’t it? Take a look at the Raycast website here.

4. Sleek - Machine Learning Product Website Design Inspiration

In fourth place comes a ML Website design template we love at BRIX: Sleek. As its name suggests, this template is ‘sleek’. We couldn’t find a better description than that.

Sleek website is about an IDE that can integrate into many platforms, so you will see many images of the product like on other websites. That being said, something special here is the use of dark mode and light mode. The balance is perfect, the layouts fit well, and the images and illustrations give the final touch to make a wonderful ML-based IDE website.

If you are impressed with this amazing website, we can help you design and develop your next ML website product — just click here to get in touch with our ML web design agency.

Feel free to check the Sleek website here.

5. Reflect - ML Website Design Inspiration

At number five comes the stunning and modern website of Reflect, showcasing an innovative product about taking notes with an AI assistant that organize thoughts and acts as an intellectual partner.

The Reflect website design is simply innovative and wonderful. For example, it features a beautiful representation of a black hole, symbolizing the consumption of information. The website maintains a perfect structure and guidelines, making it very congruent in each section. The style is cutting-edge and techy, with many gradients, small fonts, and glassmorphism elements.

Without a doubt, this is one of the best machine learning website design inspiration on our top list.

Take a look to the website of Reflect clicking here.

6. Rask.ai - Machine Learning Landing Page Design Inspiration

In sixth place is the Rask website, which features a highly trending sharp and vibrant design.

We have previously posted many machine learning websites designs based on the dark mode style, colorful gradients, and amazing illustrations, however, if you prefer a simple website but still want a modern design, Rask is an excellent source of inspiration.

The website incorporates concepts of a portfolio design style, with large layouts, big fonts, and a mix of sans serif and serif typography. The vibrant blue color used by Rask is visually impactful and adds to the modern feel of the website.

Would you like to see the Rask website for yourself? Click here.

7. Prevalent.ai - ML Web Design Inspiration

Coming in at number seven, we have the dynamic website of Prevalent. It incorporates futuristic illustrations and large layouts to make it distinct.

This website is well-known among our ML business customers, who have mentioned it in their preferences for ML website designs inspiration, and we understand why: it inspires the future.

With its futuristic illustrations, varied layouts, vibrant colors, and sci-fi style, it is very impressive, not just on the homepage, but throughout the entire site.

Why not take a look at the Prevalent website? Click here to see it.

8. ModelAI - Machine Learning Dashboard Website Design Inspiration

In eighth place, we have the stunning, sleek, and modern website of ModelAI, showcasing their impressive ML product for developers.

The style and design of ModelAI serve as a reference for any machine learning or AI company. ModelAI uses clean layouts, complete light mode, product views, and more.

Its styles share the same balance on each page, making it a really pleasant experience to stay on the entire site. Definitively, ModelAI has a world-class machine learning design website that takes the light mode approach, creating an amazing, comfortable, and very clean ML website.

Why not take a look for yourself? Check it out here.

9. Open AI - Machine Learning Web App Design Inspiration

Securing the ninth spot, we have the website of one of the most important players in the machine learning and artificial intelligence industry: OpenAI.

It’s no secret that this company has released amazing products such as DALL-E or ChatGPT, and many people would think that the websites design would be aligned with the quality they’ve given in their products, however, the design can be a little controversial because is just too simple and clean.

We know that everyone knows what ChatGPT is, and their product interface could be awkward for many users, but their product website is simply on point. With clean layouts, big images, cutting-edge illustrations, and more, OpenAI’s website is definitely worth checking out.

See for yourself by clicking here.

10. Darkweb - ML Platform Web Design Inspiration

Last but certainly not least, we have the creative and powerful website of Darkweb, which stands out for its unique balance of colors with dark mode style.

We’ve already discussed the Sleek ML IDE website, and how it balanced dark and light modes, but in this case, Darkweb doesn’t bother with light mode; this website is completely dark mode, with a lot of colors and clean layouts.

Darkweb machine learning IDE website design is mind-blowing and a best-seller on BRIX Templates. Everyone loves its style, colors, and illustrations based on the product. We think it’s simply amazing, clean, and pop.

Sounds amazing, right? Take a look at the Darkweb website here.
