How much does integrating Jetboost costs?

Projects with a top Webflow agency that involve Jetboost can have pretty different costs depending on the complexity and project scope.

Considering the public data available online for multiple agencies and freelancers that work with Jetboost, the average hourly rates range from $100 to $150 USD per hour, meaning a full Jetboost implementation can be in the range of $2.5 to $10k USD depending on the project complexity.

How long does it take to build a custom Jetboost search?

Each project has a different timeline depending on its complexity, so considering that, it's hard to tell an exact time for any project.

That being said, as Jetboost is pretty well integrated with Webflow, so thanks to that, a typical Jetboost project can easily be done in 1-2 weeks. More complicated projects can take more than this, however this is a good average to take into account.

What's the cost to maintain a Jetboost search?

Depending on the list of features that your Jetboost project may need, you will need to pay for an individual Jetboost feature, or for a All-In-One Bundle with more/all the features. Considering this, the maintenance cost will be in the range of $9 - $29 USD per month.
