Programmatic Search Engine Optimization

What is programmatic SEO?

In a nutshell, Programmatic SEO involves using data and automation to create multiple landing pages in order to target low and medium volume SEO keywords. Our process involves utilizing tools such as Ahrefs to identify the right keywords, Airtable to gather and structure data, and Figma and Webflow to bring the landing pages to life.
